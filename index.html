Amazing Race: Collingwood Edition

Challenge 1: Collingwood Children's Farm

Find a specific animal at the farm (e.g., a cow named Daisy) and take a photo with it. Check the box once you have completed this task.

Clue: Head to a place where artists thrive, and creativity is alive. Find the mural with the largest bird and take a photo with it.

Challenge 2: Street Art Hunting

Find the mural featuring the largest bird and take a selfie with it. Check the box once you have completed this task.

Clue: For a taste of local history, walk to the oldest working brewery in Melbourne and enjoy a soft drink or juice. After your refreshing break, you’ll get your next clue.

Challenge 3: Victoria Park

Complete a short trivia quiz about the history of Victoria Park and the Collingwood Football Club. Check the box once you have completed this task.

Clue: Head to the heart of Smith Street, where food and flavors meet. Find a bakery and buy any sweet treat. Show your receipt to get your next clue.

Challenge 4: Smith Street Bakery Challenge

Buy and eat a sweet treat. Keep the receipt as proof and check the box once you have completed this task.

Clue: You’re near the finish line, but not quite done. Head to where the whole community runs. Find the local market, and there you will see, your final clue under the marquee.

Challenge 5: Collingwood Yards

Find a specific booth or vendor at the market and take a photo with it. Check the box once you have completed this task.

Final Clue: Congratulations! You’ve completed your race. To celebrate, head to the end of the chase. Your final destination is the Collingwood Public Library, where you’ll find your prize.
